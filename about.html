About

Once Upon a Table is about baking fairytales and we take pride in making thoughtful desserts and cakes for any occasion. We offer a variety of desserts ranging from celebration cakes, macarons, cupcakes, tarts, jellies and truff les, all made by hand using the finest ingredients like Valrhona chocolate, French butter and Madagascan vanilla bean.


Everything began in 2015 with Fides & Thea’s shared love baking and collecting tableware. Our clients include Céline, Chanel Beauty, Nespresso and Stuart Weitzman.
